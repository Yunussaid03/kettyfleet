# 🚛 KettyFleet ERP System

> A modern fleet operations and compliance management system built for tour operators managing real-world vehicle logistics, job dispatching, compliance tracking, and operational reporting.

---

## ⚡ Vision

KettyFleet ERP is designed to centralize and digitize fleet operations for tour companies by combining vehicle management, compliance tracking, job dispatch (CDO system), maintenance monitoring, fuel efficiency analytics, and executive reporting into a single intelligent platform.

The system is built to replace fragmented spreadsheets with a structured, role-based operational workflow.

---

## 🧠 Core Philosophy

- Vehicle-centric architecture
- Job-based operational tracking (CDO system)
- Real-time operational visibility
- Data-driven decision making for fleet owners
- Clean, white-mode business UI for office environments

---

## 🏗️ System Roles

### 👨‍💼 Admin
Full operational control over:
- Fleet management
- Job creation and assignment
- Compliance tracking
- Maintenance records
- Fuel and operational logs

---

### 📊 Owner
Strategic-level visibility:
- Fleet overview dashboard
- Compliance status summary
- Cost and fuel analytics
- Monthly operational reports

---

### 🧾 Staff (Master Role)
Operational data input:
- Daily vehicle status updates
- Mileage and usage reporting
- Issue and incident reporting
- Location and activity logs

---

## 🚚 Core Modules (System Design)

### 1. Fleet Management
Central registry of all vehicles including:
- Vehicle identity (internal + registration)
- Category classification (SUV, Bus, Van, etc.)
- Operational status tracking

---

### 2. Compliance System
Tracks all regulatory requirements:
- Insurance
- NTSA Inspection
- Speed Governor Certification
- Road Service License (RSL)
- Document expiry alerts & uploads

---

### 3. Job (CDO) System
The operational backbone of the platform:
- Job creation and assignment
- Vehicle + driver allocation
- Client-based job tracking
- Trip lifecycle monitoring

---

### 4. Driver Operations
- Trip execution tracking
- Allowance and expense logging
- Job-based reconciliation

---

### 5. Maintenance Management
- Repair and servicing records
- Workshop tracking
- Cost analysis per vehicle
- Maintenance history logs

---

### 6. Fuel & Efficiency Tracking
- Fuel consumption logs
- Odometer-based tracking
- KM/L efficiency calculations
- Cost monitoring per vehicle

---

### 7. Executive Dashboard
- Fleet health overview
- Compliance alerts
- Cost summaries
- Operational performance metrics

---

## 🧱 Architecture

- Frontend: React + Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth + Storage)
- Deployment: Vercel + Supabase Cloud

---

## 📊 Key Concept: Job-Centric System

All operational data flows through a central concept:

> Every trip, expense, and activity is linked to a Job (CDO)

This ensures full traceability across fleet operations.
